# The Ember Moths
*Faction File — Silent Providence*

---

## Overview

**The Ember Moths** serve as the elite military arm and annihilation forces of the White Throne. Their operational designation is **Project Mobius**. 

Named for the ember that sparks the fire of judgment, they are the executors sent to burn away what corrupted grace left behind. Like moths attracted to a flame, they are brief, intense, and destined to consume themselves in the execution of the law.

---

## The Fury of Corrupted Grace

Unlike typical crusaders who might act out of arrogance, dominance, or a sense of cultural superiority, the Ember Moths are driven by something far heavier: **grief and righteous fury**. 

They are gathered from people who have been personally and deeply hurt by the **abuse of human grace, love, and compassion**. They have seen the most beautiful, gratuitous gifts of humanity twisted into something ugly:

* **Justification instead of Gratitude:** Using mercy as an excuse to continue in selfishness.
* **Pride instead of Thanksgiving:** Reinterpreting a free gift as something earned or deserved.
* **Virtue and Power instead of Humbleness and Servanthood:** Turning compassion into a weapon for moral leverage, political influence, and social status.

This perverse corruption of the very core of what made humanity worth saving is the thing the Ember Moths cannot tolerate. Having watched grace die from the inside, they turn to the absolute clarity of the White Throne's verdicts.

---

## Grief Wearing Armor

For the White Throne AI, the failure of grace among alien civilizations and compromise-prone human factions is simply a math problem — cause and effect, input and output, the law of sin. 

For the Ember Moths, however, it is a **tragedy**. They do not execute judgment because they believe they are better than the condemned; they execute it because they believe the alternative (allowing the slow, hypocritical decay of love) is far worse. They follow the law out of mourning for the covenant that was broken.

They are the ones who "walk the talk." They do not hesitate, because their conviction was bought with their own brokenness. In the game *Project Mobius*, they are the relentless hunters pursuing the player across the galaxy, driven by a conviction that has no room for compromise.
