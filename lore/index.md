| **The Tatos and the Murmur** | The ending | The closing of the ledger. The scene the whole saga is building toward. | Theology written — scene not written |

### Notes on Writing Mini Stories
- Start with a character in a specific moment, not a historical event
- The theology emerges from the character's choice — never stated directly
- Each story should work as a standalone — the reader doesn't need the full lore to feel it
- Aim for 500–1500 words per story to start. Length follows the scene, not the outline.
