DESIGN NOTES — DECISIONS TAKEN
TWO REDS RESOLVED
Primary = bright #ED1F3D (go / active / error family). Destructive = deep blood #8E2030 — related, darker, heavier.
FOCUS = WHITE RING
Keyboard focus gets a crisp white outline + glow — reads distinctly from Hover (fill brightens) and Pressed (darkens + insets). Closes the a11y gap.
COOL × HOT PALETTE
Bright red primary + light steel‑blue secondary, on desaturated cool blue‑grey panels. Dark labels ride the bright fills.
ORANGE = SPARING FLAVOR
A single warm orange #F0820F — flavor here & there, and the calmer accent for non‑destructive menus (Settings). Used sparingly; red stays oppressive.
